Kenya Urban Roads Authority (KURA) Meeting

The event took place at Eton Hotel on 9th October 2023.

RSAK participated in the stakeholders' consultation meeting organized by the Kenya Urban Roads Authority (KURA) for the preparation of the Non-Motorised Transport (NMT) Master Plan and the preliminary and detailed engineering design for Thika Town.
