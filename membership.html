Why Join Us?

Over 3,000 lives are lost on our roads annually, and majority of this number is drawn from our workforce. It is a fact that, millions are lost annually in trying to mitigate the effects of road accidents. This can either be through paying high insurance premium, hospital bills, loss of man-hours and valuable stored knowledge.

By joining RSAK:

  • Your organization becomes a driving force in bringing sanity on our roads.
  • Your problems and concerns are addressed on a personal level.
  • Your opinions and insight are heard and collectively used to enact a road safety bill that is expected to be debated in parliament.
  • You also benefit in the in-house training and awareness creation by our panel of experts.

Membership Advantages

Join Our Effective Road Safety Team

Road Safety Association of Kenya membership includes a growing number of organizations from the corporate sector and civil society. All of whom share a drive to collaborate in order to improve road safety in Kenya.

Members of RSAK are part of a nationally recognized network of like minded organizations committed to improving road safety, both within their organization and beyond the business gates. Being a member brings a number of benefits that help organizations achieve their sustainability goals on road safety.

Whether you come from the private sector or civil society, Road Safety Association of Kenya offers you a trusted platform for road safety by-:

Gain insight into the latest thinking and experience in areas of road safety, including research and leading examples of replicable good practice.

Collaborate with business pears across your value chain and from other industries to develop and scale up solutions to shared road safety challenges.

Tap into an extensive multi-sector network of partners to develop and implement road safety projects in communities where you operate. In particular positions within local partners that are embedded in communities and national governments.

Access to a members only resource center.

Demonstrate leadership on road safety, profile your successful programmes and strengthen your company's reputation.

Right to use the RSAK brand.

Invitation to participate in RSAK events and seminar.

Participate in policy development and advocacy efforts to influence the wider road safety environment for your business.

Access to “on-call” globally recognized road safety expertise.
