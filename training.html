Training Institutions

Training Institutions

They support training, education and development programs to produce safe and qualified drivers.

The following can help maintain road safety:

Certified Institutions

They should be fully accreditied by the relevant authorities

Professional Instructors

Teachers who are certified to instruct students on skills related to operating vehicles, including driving safety

Comprehensive Driving Lessons

They should touch on theory, skills training and proper practical driving test

Fully Equipped Training Facility

They should be up to date with the latest technology and have the relevant training materials
