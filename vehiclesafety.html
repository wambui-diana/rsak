Vehicle Safety

The Use of Digital Speed Limiters

In Kenya, speed limiters are a mandatory requirement for all Commercial Vehicles (with tare weight exceeding 3 tons) and Passenger Service Vehicles (PSVs). The KS 2295-1:2018 standard specifies requirements for the performance and installation of devices designed to limit the maximum road speed of motor vehicles by control of engine power.

Good Vehicle Conditions

Vehicle maintenance is crucial to keeping you, your family, and the people around safe. In combination with defensive driving, ensuring that your vehicle is well-maintained can prevent breakdowns and other system failures that may lead to a serious car accident.

Ensure that you do the following:

  • Brake Maintenance
  • Tire Maintenance
  • Working Vehicle Lights
